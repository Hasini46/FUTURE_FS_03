Our Story

More Than Coffee.
A Community Cup.

Ember & Bean began when two friends, Maya and Raj, decided that their neighbourhood deserved a place that felt like home — warm, unhurried, and genuinely good. We source our beans directly from farms in Coorg, Araku, and Nilgiris, and every recipe on our menu was tested at Maya's kitchen table.

Five years later, we're still that same kitchen-table café — just a little bigger, a little louder, and loved by thousands of regulars who know us by name.
